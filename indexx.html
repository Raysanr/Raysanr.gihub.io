Mission

Successfully launch the AI Sales Agent on SH Partners Facebook Page and fully automate the inquiry-to-order process — from first message to confirmed sale.



This roadmap outlines what will happen at each phase, what we need from our partners, and the measurable results they can expect at every milestone.

Instant Inquiry Response
AI answers every Facebook message the moment it arrives — no wait time, no missed leads.

Implementation Roadmap

THE 5 PHASES

A structured, step-by-step approach to launching your AI Sales System with precision and confidence.

01
Phase One
Preparation & Access
What We Need From Partners
  • Facebook Page Admin Access
  • OpenAI Subscription (API Key)
What We Will Do
  • Connect your Facebook Page to our AI system
Outcome Your page is connected and ready for AI setup.
02
Phase Two
AI Setup & Configuration
What We Will Configure
  • Automatic replies to inquiries
  • Product explanation & promo offers
  • Order detail collection (Name, Number, Address, Landmark, Quantity)
  • Order summary & confirmation step
  • Automatic order creation after confirmation
  • Smart upselling after order
  • Follow-up for incomplete address, missing landmark, unconfirmed orders

We tailor the conversation flow to match your brand voice and sales style.

Outcome Fully configured AI Sales System ready for testing.
03
Phase Three
Testing & Quality Check
What We Test Before Going Live
  • Inquiry handling
  • Order detail collection
  • Order confirmation process
  • Auto-Create Order
  • Upselling flow
  • Follow-up reminders
  • Error handling

We simulate real customer scenarios to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Outcome AI is tested and optimized.
04
Phase Four
Go Live
The AI Sales Agent Will Now
  • Answer inquiries instantly (24/7)
  • Convert inquiries into orders
  • Collect and validate customer details
  • Create confirmed orders automatically
  • Follow up on incomplete details
  • Upsell to increase average order value
Outcome Your AI Sales Agent is live and generating orders.
05
Phase Five · Ongoing
Monitoring & Optimization
After Launch We Will
  • Weekly Review AI Operations Master Tracker - Ticketing System
  • Test and Approve Recommendations from SH partners.

AI will continuously improve based on customer interaction patterns.

Outcome A self-improving system that compounds results over time.
